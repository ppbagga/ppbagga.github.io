About Us

We are a medical device company focused on improving the quality of life of patients, efficiently and effectively, for individuals at risk of pressure injuries. Instead of providing a band-aid solution to pressure injury management, Curiato Inc. provides caregivers and patients a holistic, end-to-end, management system, that leverages cloud, IoT and artificial intelligence in the form of a smart mattress and predictive healthcare platform.

Services

Curiato provide institutions with the ability to remotely monitor and manage patients at risk of developing pressure injuries, with both our mobile patient management interface and adaptive smart mattress. Our system will enable institutions to create resource-efficient planning and management of pressure injuries.
Our smart mattress system provides insights and assists clinicians based off of continuous sensing, which automatically aggregates data to build correlation, and ultimately insight creation, to assist clinicians in the decision making process.
